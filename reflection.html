REFLECTION

During the first academic quarter of this school year, many challenges were faced and solved. Along the way, I would always face difficulties and errors. I tend to overestimate my abilities and get distracted easily. I would usually cram my projects thinking that the deadline is days or even weeks away.

This upcoming second quarter, I aim to develop my study habits while still having fun and doing the things I love most. I will also take my deadlines and tests more seriously because that will lead to my overall improvement. I will make this second quarter the first step to my overall improvement in terms of academics and my daily life.
